A letter to you

From Gurman, to Simran.

Simran, I am writing this to say something simple and true. I love you. I love the way your voice softens my worries, the way your laughter finds me even on the days that feel heavy. I love the steadiness you bring to my life and the gentle courage you give me to be better. With you, ordinary moments have a quiet brightness. A walk, a call, a shared look,each one becomes a small piece of home.

When I think about us, I do not reach for grand promises. I reach for the everyday: showing up, listening well, holding your hand when the world is loud, cheering for you when you doubt, respecting you in every choice, and choosing you again and again. I want to learn you for a lifetime,the stories you have not told yet, the dreams you are still shaping, the places we will discover together.

I do not love you with fireworks; I love you with a steady flame. It is warm, patient, and honest. It makes room for both your strength and your softness. I am grateful for your trust, for your kindness, and for the way you let me see you as you are. I am here, today and all the days to come. Thank you for being my person. I love you.
